AEDP - Attraction Economics Design Planning,  is a boutique consulting practice focusing on theme park feasibility studies, strategic advisory and management consulting with a strong operational background. 


AEDP has been in forefront of strategic advisory, planning and economic consulting for new generation visitor attraction concepts for the past two decades. 


The AEDP team has worked on projects big and small in   

Australia, China, Colombia, Germany, Hong Kong, Indonesia, Kenya, Macau, Malaysia, Morocco, Norway, Portugal, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Singapore, South Korea, Sweden, Switzerland, Thailand, The Netherlands, Tunisia, Turkey, United Arab Emirates and Vietnam. 


  At present AEDP is working with several international attractions in Asia, Europe, South America and the Middle East
